Country Boil

Whole Spice Packet

Mustard Seed, Coriander Seed, Cayenne Pepper, Bay Leaves, Dill Seed, Allspice

Wrap in cheesecloth or tea ball.

Steps

  • Fill a large pot with water.
  • Salt heavily
  • Add spice packet, or commercial boil-in-bag.
  • Add healthy amount Old Bay.
  • Add ground red pepper of choice for spiciness.
  • Add halved lemons.
  • Bring all to a boil.
For the following, add then let boil for the time following. Iterating down the list. Anything can be omitted, but boil for it's time.
  • Onions, 5 minutes
  • Red potatoes and chicken thighs, 20 minutes.
  • Sausage, chicken breasts or tenderloins, crawfish, 10 minutes.
  • Corn, 10 minutes
  • Turn off heat add shrimp.
