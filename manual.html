I. The Story

You're fourteen, small for your age, and at the bottom of every food chain that matters. The block's got a pecking order — and right now you're somewhere below the pigeons.

But today's gonna be different.

Fight your way up from a nobody kid on a busted block to the Boss Level — running the entire city. Eight chapters. Eight bosses. One long climb from fists to laser launchers.

Every choice matters. Every fight has consequences. Some shopkeepers shoot back. The cops don't play fair. And there's always someone bigger waiting at the top.

II. How to Play

Starting the Game

Open a terminal and run:

COMMAND
cd src
python main.py

Requires Python 3.8+. No external dependencies needed.

Navigation

Type a number to select from the menu. You can travel between locations, talk to NPCs, visit shops, pick fights, or use items. Each chapter opens a new hub area with connected locations.

Exploration

Moving between locations may trigger random encounters, environmental hazards, police patrols, or ambient events. Stay alert — the streets don't wait for you to get ready.

WARNING
There is no save feature. When you die, you start over.
Choose your fights carefully.

III. Combat

Combat is turn-based. You and your opponent trade blows until one of you drops to 0 HP — or you run away.

Actions

Action Damage Requirement
Weak Punch 1–3 + weapon bonus Always available
Strong Punch 4–8 + weapon bonus Fighter rank (10+ respect)
Run Away — Always available (may fail)

Weapons

Weapons add a flat damage bonus to every attack. Your best weapon auto-equips when picked up. You can't un-equip a weapon — only replace it with a stronger one.

Combat Results

ResultEffect
WIN Earn respect + possible money/item drops.
LOSE Lose some HP. Game over if you hit 0.
RUN Escape to the hub. No rewards.
SCRIPTED LOSS Story-required defeat. You survive but learn a lesson.
TIP
Boss fights often start with a scripted loss. Don't waste healing items — you're supposed to lose the first round. Come back with better gear.

VII. Game Systems

🔫 Store Owner Fight-Back

Not every shopkeeper is a pushover. Some fight back when you try to rob them — swinging bats, pulling shotguns, or worse. Each shopkeeper has a fight-back chance and damage range. Some carry lethal weapons with a small instant-death probability.

WARNING
The ☠ symbol means instant death is possible. If the death roll hits, it's game over — no HP check, no second chance. The risk increases with each chapter.

🚔 Police System

Starting in Chapter 2, police patrol the streets. Every time you move between locations, there's a chance of encountering a patrol. If you're caught doing something illegal, you get arrested — losing a percentage of your cash and a chunk of respect.

VIII. Tips & Secrets

TIP #1
Save your healing items for boss fights. Street enemies drop loot — bosses don't give you a second chance.
TIP #2
Talk to every NPC before attempting a boss fight. Info NPCs like Diesel Dave, Barkeep Ray, and Fish Monger Sal give critical intel that makes fights easier.
TIP #3
When you buy the brass knuckles, look for the screwdriver on the counter. You'll need it to steal the car stereo. Items that seem small now might be quest-critical later.
TIP #4
Police get worse every chapter. In Chapters 7 and 8, getting arrested can wipe 60–70% of your cash. Stay low-profile when your wallet's fat.
TIP #5
Armed shopkeepers can kill you instantly. In Chapter 5, the armed shopkeeper enemy has an 8% death chance. That's almost 1 in 12. Don't get greedy.
TIP #6
The laser launcher in Chapter 7 requires three components from different locations. You absolutely need it for the boss fight — Viktor Kessler's exo-suit is immune to conventional weapons.
TIP #7
In Chapter 8, NPCs from previous chapters return to help you. The Prophet, Nails, Sparks, and Ghost all have roles in the final assault on the Kingpin's stronghold.
SECRET
The Old Block location in Chapter 8 is a callback to Chapter 1. Visit it before the final fight for a moment of reflection — and maybe a hidden item.
